A thousand and one stories is powered by writers from around the world. We review submissions and bring them to an audience of readers. If you love to write, submit a story! Whether you've had work published or not, why not give it a shot? Whether we decide to put your work up on the site or not, we will give you [free?] feedback on your writing. Send your stories to thousandonestories@gmail.com and create an account to monitor its progress.
